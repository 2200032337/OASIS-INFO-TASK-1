Artificial Intelligence


Artificial Intelligence focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem-solving, understanding natural language. AI is divided into various subfields, such as machine learning, which enables systems to learn from data and natural language processing, which allows computers to understand and generate human language. The ultimate goal of AI is to develop machines that can think and act autonomously. Register
